Phoenix

The Fiery Duelist

Hailing from the UK, Phoenix's star power shines through in his fighting style, igniting the battlefield with flash and flare. Whether he's got backup or not, he'll rush into a fight on his own terms.

  • Role: Duelist
  • Origin: United Kingdom
  • Release Date: June 2, 2020
  • Signature Ability: Hot Hands

Abilities

Blaze (C)

Cast a flame wall that blocks vision and damages players passing through it. Can be curved while casting.

Cost: 200 Credits

Curveball (Q)

Throw a flare orb that curves and detonates shortly after throwing, blinding enemies.

Cost: 250 Credits

Hot Hands (E)

Throw a fireball that explodes after a delay, creating a lingering fire zone that heals Phoenix and damages enemies.

Cost: Free (1 charge)

Run It Back (X)

Mark your current location. If you die during this ability's duration, you'll respawn at the marked location with full health.

Cost: 6 Ultimate Points
