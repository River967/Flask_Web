Omen

The Phantom Stealth

A mysterious entity who manipulates darkness and teleports across the battlefield, Omen hunts enemies with brutal efficiency. His shadowy form allows him to strike unseen and disappear without a trace.

  • Role: Controller
  • Origin: Unknown
  • Release Date: June 2, 2020
  • Signature Ability: Dark Cover

Abilities

Shrouded Step (C)

After a delay, teleport a short distance to the targeted location.

Cost: 100 Credits

Paranoia (Q)

Send out an ethereal shadow in a straight line, nearsighting anyone it touches.

Cost: 200 Credits

Dark Cover (E)

Cast out a shadow sphere that expands into a obscuring sphere of darkness at its location. Can be charged to increase range.

Cost: Free (2 charges)

From the Shadows (X)

Select anywhere on the map to teleport and reform. When arriving, appear as a shadow that can be destroyed to cancel the teleport.

Cost: 7 Ultimate Points
