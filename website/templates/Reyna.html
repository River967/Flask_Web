Reyna

The Vampiric Duelist

Forged in the heart of Mexico, Reyna dominates single combat, popping off with each kill she scores. Her capability to harvest souls and become invulnerable makes her nearly unstoppable when she's rolling.

  • Role: Duelist
  • Origin: Mexico
  • Release Date: June 2, 2020
  • Signature Ability: Devour

Abilities

Leer (C)

Equip an ethereal eye that can be activated to blind all enemies who look at it.

Cost: 250 Credits

Dismiss (Q)

Consume a nearby Soul Orb to become intangible for a short duration.

Cost: 1 Soul Orb

Devour (E)

Consume a nearby Soul Orb to heal yourself.

Cost: 1 Soul Orb

Empress (X)

Enter a frenzy, increasing firing speed, equip speed, and reload speed. Scoring a kill renews the duration.

Cost: 6 Ultimate Points
