Cypher

The Moroccan Surveillance Expert

Cypher is a one-man surveillance network who keeps tabs on the enemy's every move. No secret is safe, no maneuver goes unseen - he is the eyes and ears of any team.

  • Role: Sentinel
  • Origin: Morocco
  • Release Date: April 7, 2020
  • Signature Ability: Spycam

Abilities

Trapwire (C)

Place a covert tripwire between two walls that reveals and restrains enemies who cross it.

Cost: 200 Credits

Cyber Cage (Q)

Place a remote-activated cage that blocks vision and slows enemies who pass through.

Cost: 100 Credits

Spycam (E)

Place a remote camera that can be controlled to watch an area and fire tracking darts.

Cost: Free (1 per round)

Neural Theft (X)

Extract information from a dead enemy to reveal the location of all living enemies.

Cost: 6 Ultimate Points
