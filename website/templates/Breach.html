Breach

The Bionic Swede

Breach fires powerful, targeted kinetic blasts to aggressively clear a path through enemy ground. His mechanical arms and seismic abilities make him the perfect initiator for pushing through defended sites.

  • Role: Initiator
  • Origin: Sweden
  • Release Date: June 2, 2020
  • Signature Ability: Fault Line

Abilities

Aftershock (C)

Fire a slow-firing burst through walls that deals heavy damage to anyone caught in the area.

Cost: 200 Credits

Flashpoint (Q)

Equip a blinding charge that fires through walls, detonating to blind all players looking at it.

Cost: 250 Credits

Fault Line (E)

Send a seismic blast that dazes and knocks up anyone in its path.

Cost: Free (1 per round)

Rolling Thunder (X)

Fire a cascading earthquake that knocks up and stuns everyone in its large rectangular area.

Cost: 7 Ultimate Points
