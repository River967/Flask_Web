Raze

The Explosive Duelist

Raze explodes out of Brazil with her big personality and bigger guns. With her blunt-force-trauma playstyle, she excels at flushing entrenched enemies and clearing tight spaces with a generous dose of "boom".

  • Role: Duelist
  • Origin: Brazil
  • Release Date: June 2, 2020
  • Signature Ability: Paint Shells

Abilities

Boom Bot (C)

Deploy a bot that travels in a straight line, bouncing off walls and exploding when near enemies.

Cost: 300 Credits

Blast Pack (Q)

Throw an explosive that sticks to surfaces and can be detonated for damage or mobility.

Cost: 200 Credits

Paint Shells (E)

Equip a cluster grenade that explodes into sub-munitions, damaging enemies in range.

Cost: Free (1 charge)

Showstopper (X)

Equip a rocket launcher that fires a rocket dealing massive area damage on contact.

Cost: 8 Ultimate Points
