Skye

The Guiding Light

Hailing from Australia, Skye and her band of beasts trailblaze the way through hostile territory. With her creations hampering the enemy, and her power to heal others, the team is strongest and safest by Skye's side.

  • Role: Initiator
  • Origin: Australia
  • Release Date: October 27, 2020
  • Signature Ability: Guiding Light

Abilities

Regrowth (C)

Heal allies in range and line of sight. Can be reused until her healing pool is depleted.

Cost: 200 Credits

Trailblazer (Q)

Control a Tasmanian tiger trinket to scout for enemies and concuss those in its path.

Cost: 250 Credits

Guiding Light (E)

Summon a hawk trinket that can be guided to flash enemies in its line of sight.

Cost: Free (1 charge)

Seekers (X)

Summon three seekers that track down the three closest enemies and nearsight them.

Cost: 7 Ultimate Points
