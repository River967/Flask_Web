Jett

The Agile Duelist

Representing her home country of South Korea, Jett's agile and evasive fighting style lets her take risks no one else can. She runs circles around every skirmish, cutting enemies before they even know what hit them.

  • Role: Duelist
  • Origin: South Korea
  • Release Date: June 2, 2020
  • Signature Ability: Tailwind

Abilities

Cloudburst (C)

Throw a cloud of fog that obscures vision on impact. Can be curved mid-flight.

Cost: 150 Credits

Updraft (Q)

Instantly propel Jett high into the air for elevated vantage points.

Cost: 150 Credits

Tailwind (E)

Dash a short distance in the direction Jett is moving. Recharges after 2 kills.

Cost: Free (1 charge)

Blade Storm (X)

Equip a set of throwing knives that recharge on kills. Alternate fire throws all remaining daggers.

Cost: 7 Ultimate Points
