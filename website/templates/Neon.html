Neon

The Radiant Rush

Filipino Agent Neon surges forward at shocking speeds, discharging bursts of bioelectric radiance as fast as her body generates it. She races ahead to catch enemies off guard, then strikes them down quicker than lightning.

  • Role: Duelist
  • Origin: Philippines
  • Release Date: January 12, 2022
  • Signature Ability: High Gear

Abilities

Fast Lane (C)

Fire two energy lines forward that create walls of static electricity, blocking vision and damaging enemies passing through.

Cost: 300 Credits

Relay Bolt (Q)

Throw an energy bolt that bounces once, stunning enemies near its impact point.

Cost: 200 Credits

High Gear (E)

Gain increased speed. When charged, alternate fire to slide. Slide resets on kills.

Cost: Free (1 charge)

Overdrive (X)

Unleash Neon's full power for a short duration. Fire to channel her power into a deadly lightning beam.

Cost: 7 Ultimate Points
