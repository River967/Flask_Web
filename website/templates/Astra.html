Astra

The Cosmic Controller

Astra bends the cosmos to reshape battlefields to her whim. With cosmic powers from her Ghanaian heritage, she places stars that can be activated for devastating effects, making her the ultimate strategic controller.

  • Role: Controller
  • Origin: Ghana
  • Release Date: March 2, 2021
  • Signature Ability: Astral Form

Abilities

Gravity Well (C)

Place a Star that pulls players toward center before exploding, making them fragile.

Cost: 150 Credits

Nova Pulse (Q)

Place a Star that concusses all players in area after a brief delay.

Cost: 150 Credits

Nebula (E)

Transform a Star into a smoke or temporarily remove it to reposition.

Cost: Free (1 per round)

Cosmic Divide (X)

Create a massive wall that blocks bullets and heavily dampens audio.

Cost: 7 Ultimate Points
