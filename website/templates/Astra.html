Astra

The Cosmic Controller

Astra is an Agent in Valorant, hailing from Ghana. She is a Controller who uses her cosmic powers to reshape the battlefield, placing stars and activating them for different effects. Astra excels at map control and strategic play, making her a favorite for coordinated teams.

  • Role: Controller
  • Origin: Ghana
  • Release Date: March 2, 2021

Abilities

C - Gravity Well

Place a Star. Activate to pull players toward the center before it explodes, making all players in the area fragile.

Q - Nova Pulse

Place a Star. Activate to concuss all players in the area after a brief delay.

E - Nebula / Dissipate

Place a Star. Activate to transform it into a smoke (Nebula). Dissipate temporarily removes a Star, which can be placed again after a delay.

X - Astral Form / Cosmic Divide

Enter Astral Form to place Stars. Use Cosmic Divide to create a massive wall that blocks bullets and dampens audio.
