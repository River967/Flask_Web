Killjoy

The Genius Sentinel

A brilliant inventor from Germany, Killjoy secures the battlefield with her arsenal of gadgets. Her traps and turrets make short work of anyone foolish enough to push her territory.

  • Role: Sentinel
  • Origin: Germany
  • Release Date: August 4, 2020
  • Signature Ability: Turret

Abilities

Alarmbot (C)

Deploy a bot that hunts down enemies in range and explodes, applying Vulnerable to them.

Cost: 200 Credits

Nanoswarm (Q)

Throw a grenade that deploys a damaging swarm of nanobots. Can be activated remotely.

Cost: 200 Credits

Turret (E)

Deploy a turret that fires at enemies in a 180-degree cone. Can be recalled and redeployed.

Cost: Free (1 charge)

Lockdown (X)

Deploy a device that detains all enemies in its radius after a long windup.

Cost: 7 Ultimate Points
